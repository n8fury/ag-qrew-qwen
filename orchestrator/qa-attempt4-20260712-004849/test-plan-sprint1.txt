1. OBJECTIVE
------------
The objective of this sprint is to deliver a functional authentication system and task management capabilities for the Demo Task Manager. QA must verify that users can sign in with their roles, and authenticated users can manage tasks (list, create, update, delete) with proper validation and error handling.
